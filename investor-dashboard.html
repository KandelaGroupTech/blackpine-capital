Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
Click the "Reset Password" button in the top right corner. You'll receive an email with instructions to create a new password.
How do I download documents?
Navigate to the Documents tab and click the "Download" button next to any document you wish to save to your device.
When will I receive tax documents?
Tax documents (K-1 forms) are typically available by March 15th each year. You'll receive a notification when they're ready to download.
Who do I contact for questions?
For any questions or concerns, please reply to any message in your Messages tab, or contact us directly at info@theblackpinegroup.com.
How often are financial statements updated?
Financial statements are updated quarterly and will be available in the Documents section within 45 days of quarter end.
