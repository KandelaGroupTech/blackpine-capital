ABOUT US

Blackpine Capital is a strategic partner focused on acquiring and building leading healthcare and business services platform companies in the lower middle market. Leveraging the extensive backgrounds, experiences, and networks of our Partners and Advisors, we utilize a proactive, data-driven investment process to provide business owners with capital, business development expertise, and industry knowledge.

Blackpine Capital builds partnerships with experienced managers aiming to grow their business. We offer support and resources while respecting their independence in running day-to-day operations.

Our approach centers on identifying and supporting growth opportunities through a flexible model that aligns with the needs of our partners and stakeholders.

OUR TEAM

RICHARD ASALA

Managing Partner

As the Managing Partner of Blackpine Capital, Richard oversees investment sourcing and valuation, transaction structuring, acquisition financing, fundraising, and value creation strategies. Richard brings over a decade of experience in private equity, strategy and financial services.

Before founding Blackpine Capital, Richard worked as an investment professional at Audax Private Equity, a Boston-based private equity firm managing approximately $27 billion in AUM, where he covered healthcare and business services. Prior to his role at Audax, Richard was with Morgan Stanley in their Healthcare Investment Banking group in New York City.

He began his professional journey at Artisan Healthcare Consulting, strategy consulting services to the life sciences industy.

Richard holds a B.E. in Electrical Engineering from Dartmouth College and holds an MBA from The Tuck School of Business at Dartmouth College.
